About

Lets take a look back in time.. A brief history of me

  • August 2017

    Humble Beginnings

    Graduated with a Degree in Supply Chain Management from Auburn University and a Minor in Human Sciences.

  • Later that August 2017

    A Supply Chain Fein

    Shortly after graduation I started working as a Transportation Solutions Representative at Veritiv Corp. Where I communicated with suppliers and negotiated contracts with transportation companies.

  • May 2017

    Phase One: Transition to the Web World

    I started getting a itch to be creative, so I taught myself after work how to use Adobe Creative Cloud Tools to make Graphic Designs. This simulatiously sparked an interest in Website Design and Coding. Launched my freelancing business Authentic Design Co. Took on clients after work and on the weekends to grow my expertise in Web Design.

  • August 2018

    Phase Two: Coding Bootcamp

    After some small success with my Branding and Web Design Business I transitioned to a full time career in Website Development and enrolled in GA Tech Coding Bootcamp. I've been Coding and Creating ever since!
